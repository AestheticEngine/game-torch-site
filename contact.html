Player support

For bugs, crashes, installation issues, or general help:
support@aestheticengine.games

Helpful details to include (if you can):

  • Your OS (Windows version, if known)
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • Any on-screen error text
  • Your Game Torch version (as shown in-game or on Steam)
  • Optional: attach logs/screenshots if you’re comfortable sharing them

Safety note: We will never ask for your Steam password or payment details.
